Frequently Asked Questions

I love hearing recipe requests from my community! Send me an email at hello@thenourishedbite.com with your idea, dietary preferences, and any specific ingredients you'd like me to feature. I read every request and often create recipes based on your suggestions.

Yes! I offer both virtual and in-person cooking workshops focusing on healthy meal preparation, knife skills, and nutrition education. Check my Instagram for upcoming workshop announcements, or email me for private group sessions.

I'm happy to have you share my recipes! Please credit The Nourished Bite and include a link back to the original recipe. For commercial use or republishing, please reach out to discuss proper licensing.

Every recipe goes through extensive testing to ensure it's both nutritious and delicious. I focus on whole foods, balanced macronutrients, and flavors that make healthy eating enjoyable. Each recipe is tested multiple times and often includes input from my community.

I carefully select brand partnerships that align with my values of promoting healthy, sustainable eating. If you're interested in collaborating, please send your proposal to partnerships@thenourishedbite.com with details about your brand and how we might work together.
